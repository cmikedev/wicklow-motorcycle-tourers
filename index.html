Welcome!

Your journey starts here

We are Wicklow's largest motorcycle touring group open to all licence and bike types.

From our starting point in Laragh at the meeting between the stunning Glenmacnass Waterfall and Glendalough, our weekly adventures begin.

Come join us and discover Wicklow's mountains, valleys, rivers and green lanes.

Let's Ride!

We are the largest non-member motorcycle touring group outside of Dublin

For over 25 years we have been traversing the riding nirvanas of the gaps, the glens, the forests, the lakes and mountains that make up Wicklow, Ireland's garden.

Non-member means that anyone with any licence or bike type is welcome to join us. There is are no requirements other than to show up to our meeting point in Laragh and be prepared for a day of adventure.
