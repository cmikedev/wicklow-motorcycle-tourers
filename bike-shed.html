The Bike Shed!

We might not have premises but we have lots of experience in the group! We have many riders who turn up at the weekend sans-bike seeking to pick the brains of the more mechanically-minded over a coffee.

Whilst there's no capacity to tinker with bikes at our meeting point in Laragh, many of our riders spend part of the day getting advice on issues, modifications and maintenance.

Best of all, there will always be riders around after the group ride who will be able to answer any of your questions and offer advice so it really isn't an either or!

Sign-up now and get to know your bike inside and out as well as the Garden of Ireland.
