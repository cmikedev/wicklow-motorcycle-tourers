Our Rides

Weather permitting, we do two group rides per week starting from our meeting point in Laragh, Co. Wicklow. The details of our rides including location can be found below.

We are more than happy for anyone to come along along, however, not all rides are suitable for everyone. Some rides involve motorways or some mildly technical green lanes that may not be ideal for your skill, licence or indeed bike type.

Generally, routes are ad hoc and cater to the skill level of the riders that show up on the day. Some rides are planned in advance, particularly in the summer. Where this is the case we will send out a route plan via email and so we would strongly encourage you to visit our sign-up page whereby you'll be kept up to date of any planned rides or cancellations.
